
================================================================================
FINQA: STRATEGIC ROADMAP (In case of extended GPU Access)
================================================================================
Target: Enhancing Reasoning Depth and Operational Precision
Proposed Timeline: 1 Week (GPU-Accelerated)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PARAMETER-EFFICIENT FINE-TUNING (PEFT/LoRA)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The current 0.5B model is limited by its lack of familiarity with the specific 
Domain-Specific Language (DSL) used in FinQA programs. 
- Strategy: Use QLoRA (Quantized Low-Rank Adaptation) to fine-tune specifically 
  on the 'gold_program' sequences.
- Objective: Resolve 'Operation Errors' where the model identifies correct 
  numerical variables but applies the wrong operator or argument order.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. DIRECT PREFERENCE OPTIMIZATION (DPO) WITH EXECUTION FEEDBACK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Align the model's internal weights with logical validity rather than surface-
level text matching.
- Strategy: Use the FinQA execution environment as a reward signal. Generate 
  candidate programs and apply DPO to 'punish' programs resulting in execution 
  errors and 'reward' those matching the exact gold answer.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. SPECIALIZED CROSS-ENCODER RETRIEVER
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Reclaim the model's 'Instruction Budget' by offloading retrieval to a smaller, 
highly specialized component.
- Strategy: Train a lightweight Cross-Encoder (e.g., DistilBERT-sized) to rank 
  the relevance of table rows and text sentences against the query.
- Objective: Feed only the Top-N relevant 'facts' to the LLM, allowing the 0.5B 
  parameters to focus entirely on mathematical synthesis.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. KNOWLEDGE DISTILLATION FROM A TEACHER MODEL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Embed high-level reasoning paths from significantly larger architectures into 
the smaller model.
- Strategy: Use a 'Teacher' model (e.g., Qwen-72B or GPT-4o) to generate 
  detailed Chain-of-Thought (CoT) reasoning traces. 
- Objective: Train the 0.5B model to mimic these logic paths, distilling 
  reasoning density into the efficient architecture.
